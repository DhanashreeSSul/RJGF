Welcome to Raveendra Jadhav Global Foundation

Inspired by the legacy of Late Shri Raveendra Jadhav, we are dedicated to uplifting communities through compassion, integrity, and sustainable action. Join us in our journey to create a healthier, more educated, and greener world for all.

Our Foundation Pillars

Guiding principles that drive our initiatives

Vision

To create a sustainable and equitable society by empowering the less privileged through healthcare, education, and environmental sustainability.

Mission

To provide essential support in healthcare, environmental conservation, and education through community-driven initiatives and partnerships.
