Why Education Matters

Education is the cornerstone of a progressive society. It empowers individuals to break the cycle of poverty, improves health outcomes, and boosts economic growth. Children who receive quality education are more likely to become responsible citizens and contribute meaningfully to their communities.

Education fosters creativity, critical thinking, and the skills necessary for meaningful participation in society. It not only helps children imagine a better world, but equips them to create it.

Over 35 million children in India aged 6-14 years do not attend school.
