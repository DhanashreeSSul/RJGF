Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about contacting and working with us.

We're always looking for passionate volunteers! Please send us a message through the contact form mentioning your areas of interest and availability. Our volunteer coordinator will get back to you with opportunities that match your skills and interests.

Our main office is open Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturdays from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays and public holidays. For field projects, timings may vary based on the activity.

We appreciate your generosity! You can donate through various methods including online transfers, UPI, or cheques. Please contact our donations team at rjglobalfoundation@gmail.com or call +91 9922954841 for more details on donation options and tax benefits.

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For more complex questions that require input from specific team members, it may take up to 48 hours. If your matter is urgent, please mention it in your message or call our helpline numbers.
